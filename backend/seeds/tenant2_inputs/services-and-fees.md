# Services and Fees

All fees below are our standard private fees. Where a treatment needs a lab
(crowns, bridges, dentures) the lab component is already included in the
figure shown - there is no separate lab bill.

## Examinations and Diagnostics

A new patient examination is 95 dollars. It is a longer appointment than a
routine check because it includes a full charting of every tooth, a gum
health assessment, an oral cancer screening, and the baseline X-rays.

A routine check-up for an existing patient is 55 dollars. Small bitewing
X-rays, when needed, are 35 dollars for the set. A full panoramic X-ray, used
when we need to see the whole jaw at once, is 90 dollars. An emergency
examination for pain, which covers diagnosis and immediate pain relief but
not the definitive treatment, is 75 dollars.

## Hygiene and Prevention

A standard scale and polish with the hygienist is 85 dollars. Patients with
established gum disease usually need deep cleaning below the gumline
instead, which we charge per quadrant of the mouth at 130 dollars per
quadrant, so a full mouth is four quadrants.

A fluoride varnish application is 30 dollars. Fissure sealants, the
protective coating placed on the biting surface of back teeth, are 45
dollars per tooth. A custom-fitted night guard for grinding is 320 dollars.

## Fillings and Restorations

A composite (white) filling is 145 dollars for a single surface and 195
dollars for a filling covering two or more surfaces. An amalgam filling,
where a patient specifically requests one, is 120 dollars.

A porcelain crown is 950 dollars. A porcelain veneer is 890 dollars. An
inlay or onlay, which is a lab-made partial crown for a tooth too damaged
for a filling but not damaged enough to need a full crown, is 780 dollars.

## Root Canal Treatment

Root canal fees depend on how many canals the tooth has. A front tooth is
620 dollars, a premolar is 740 dollars, and a molar is 920 dollars. A crown
is almost always recommended afterwards and is charged separately at the
crown fee.

## Extractions and Surgery

A simple extraction is 180 dollars. A surgical extraction, needed when the
tooth has to be sectioned or the gum lifted, is 340 dollars. Wisdom tooth
removal is 420 dollars per tooth.

## Replacing Missing Teeth

A single dental implant, including the surgical placement and the crown on
top, is 3200 dollars. A three-unit bridge is 2400 dollars. A full upper or
lower denture is 1450 dollars, and a partial denture is 980 dollars.

## Cosmetic

Take-home whitening with custom trays is 380 dollars. In-chair whitening in
a single visit is 550 dollars. A single top-up whitening gel syringe for
patients who already have trays from us is 45 dollars.

## Plans and Surcharges

The family preventive plan is 55 dollars per month for a household and
covers two check-ups and two hygiene visits per adult per year, plus
check-ups and fluoride for any registered child under 16.

Appointments booked outside our normal weekday hours - Saturday mornings and
weekday evenings after six - carry an out-of-hours surcharge of 60 dollars
on top of the treatment fee. There is no surcharge on the two emergency
slots we hold each weekday morning.
